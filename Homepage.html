SERENE STAY

WELCOME TO SERENE STAY SUITES

Self care is how you take your power back. Life can be so stressing and sometimes you can be on the verge of burning
out, especially when everything seems six feet under. Located deep in Kakamega county, away from the hustle and butsle
of the city, Serene Stay guarantees that your stay will not only leave you relaxed but also happier and recharged so that
you can go back to your hustle feeling stronger and motivated.

Serene Stay, one of the Best Luxury Resorts in Kenya, integrates traditional Ayurveda, Yoga and Vedanta with international
wellness experiences, fitness and healthy organic cuisine to restore balance and harmonise energy
. We don't allow any technological gadgets during your stay in order to disconnect from the world as we reconnect ourselves with
the spirit of tranquility and relaxation.
